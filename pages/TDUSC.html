Test Drive Unlimited

Solar Crown

In my opinion one of the biggest launch in recent years!

I have been a great fan since the original Test Drive Unlimited 1 and 2. And now, after 9 years we finally get a sequel which i am very excited about.

Features i would like to see return to the new game

From the previous Test Drive Unlimited games

Customizable houses

One of the most unique feature was that you could own your own houses, customize them however you liked and that it acted as a kind of hub of some sorts. You could read the news, watch the upcoming weather and see any messages you had. It was a nice addition that helped with the roleplay aspect that also had its purpose gameplay wise. The higher the cost of said house, the more car spaces you would get.

extensive car library

The previous 2 games had a very big car library and a few motorbikes aswel. Ofcourse some of the mayor car brands like ferrari and porsche have already been confirmed thanks to the teaser they released a few days ago. I have no doubt that the car library of the new game would be about the same as the old games, maybe even more. What also really added to the roleplay immersion was that you had to actually go to a specific car store, walk around the store. Find the car you wanted. Then you could choose between different interior designs, colors and rims. You could also testdrive the cars before buying them which was a nice option to have aswel.

Character customization

Character cusotmization was also one of the thing you could do to make your characters truly your own. If you visisted one of the cities there would be atleast 1 clothing shop.To generic tshirt to luxury suits. You could then show your clothing off at your houses when your friends were invited.

1:1 Open world islands

Test Drive Unlimited 2 had 2 1:1 islands you could drive on. Ibiza and ohau. Both islands were full of events and stores you could go to. In order to go from one island to another, you had to drive to the airport. It was an insanly good idea which also helped with the roleplay aspect of the game.
